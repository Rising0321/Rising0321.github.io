About(关于)

I am a second year Ph.D. student at Beihang SCSE. I am very fortunate to be advised by Prof. Leilei Sun.

我是来自北航计算机学院的一名博士生，我很荣幸能够被孙磊磊老师指导。

My research interests lie in large spatiotemporal model, large vision-language model, and generative models. I aim to build general urban intelligence matching or even beyond human intelligence.

我的研究兴趣点在于 时空大模型, 视觉-语言大模型, 以及生成模型. 我希望能够通过我和一众学者的努力能够构建达到甚至超越人类能力的通用城市智能。

Unfortunately, my research career is not peace. I, as the first author, have been rejected six times in a row. This may be related to my previous lack of focus on a research direction. I hope that both myself and you who see this homepage will be more determined and lucky.

不幸的是，我的研究生涯不是那么顺利。我作为第一作者的论文已经被连续拒稿6次了。这可能与我此前没有专注于一个研究方向有关。 希望我和看到这个主页的你都会更坚定，更幸运。

Marathons(参加过的马拉松)

我很喜欢跑步，因为跑步会分泌多巴胺，会让我感觉到快乐。同时，我也参与了西安怪兽跑团，跑团教会我一个人跑步心情是一种颜色，一群人跑步心情汇聚成一道彩虹。在跑团中，不断的认识新的朋友，不断的帮助新人开始他们的第一次跑步，也是一件很快乐的事情。然而，和我不幸的学术生涯类似的，我的跑步水平这些年也在不断下降。我在积极努力恢复训练，争取有一天你能在跑步的赛道上看到我。

I really enjoy running because it secretes dopamine, which makes me feel happy. I also participated in the Xi'an Monster Crew Running, which taught me that running alone is a color, but a group of people's running converges into a rainbow. In our running crew, constantly meeting new friends and helping newcomers start their first run is also a very happy thing. Unfortunately, similar to my miserable academic career, my running skills have been continuously declining over the years. I am actively working to resume training, hoping that one day you can see me on the running track
